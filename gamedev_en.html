Type of games

Our most succesful game

Our most succesful game is called Music Racer.

About the game

The genre of the game is: racing

The game is about a racing driver who can only drive fast if he listens to music while driving. By winning games you unlock more songs.

I have completed this game multiple times and I have always enjoyed playing it. It is definitely my favourite game.

Our role

Onze rol bij het ontwikkelen van de games is vooral de link tussen de graphics en de muziek. Wij zorgen er dus met name voor dat de juiste muziek bij de juiste levels word gekozen, en dat de muziek ook gelicentieerd word. Our role within the development team is mostly the link between graphics and music. We mainly take care of choosing the right music for each level and we also take care of licensing the music.

We also take care of all the sound effects, which we make ourselves.

A few examples of sound effects we make:

  • Sound effects in menus
  • Engine sounds for the cars
  • Soundeffects during pitstops
